Thailand Visa

For Foreign Residents in Korea
한국 거주 외국인을 위한 태국 비자 대행

Thailand e-Visa Application

Korean citizens do not need a visa for tourism (90 days).
Foreign residents in Korea may need an e-Visa depending on their nationality.

한국인은 무비자 입국이 가능하나, 한국에 거주하는 외국인은 국적에 따라 전자비자를 받아야 합니다.

Required Documents (구비서류)
  • Passport: Bio page scan (JPG), valid for 6+ months.
  • Photo: Recent color photo (JPG) taken within 6 months.
  • ARC: Front and Back scan of Alien Registration Card.
  • Bank Statement: English statement with sufficient balance (rec. 2,000,000 KRW+).
  • Proof of Status: Employment or Student Certificate (English).
  • Flight & Hotel: Confirmed round-trip flight and hotel bookings.
  • Verification Photo: Photo of applicant holding passport page next to face.
